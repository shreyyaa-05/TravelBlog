About Us

Hi! We’re Riya & Shreya, two college friends who decided to turn our shared passion for travel into something bigger. Our journey together started with a love for exploring new places, sharing stories, and diving into cultures that are far from the typical tourist trail.

Now, we’ve created When in Rome, We Roam as a way to bring you along on our adventures. From hidden gems in major cities to the best off-the-beaten-path destinations, we aim to share our experiences, tips, and insights to help you travel in a way that’s authentic, fun, and, above all, unforgettable.

Whether it’s taking you to Rome (or anywhere else) or offering practical advice for your next trip, we’re here to inspire you to pack your bags and roam with us. We believe in traveling not just to see the world but to experience it—making connections, embracing new perspectives, and finding moments that will stay with you forever.

So, join us on this journey. There’s a whole world out there waiting to be explored, and we can’t wait to help you make the most of it.

Our journey started with a simple dream to explore the world from above.

Every stamp in our passport tells a story of adventure, discovery, and connection.
